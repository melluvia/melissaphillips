Corporal Joshua Phillips

Musician, family man, warrior and friend



A Timeline of Joshua Phillips' Life

  • 1981 - Born in Sacramento, California
  • 2002 - Travels to Amsterdam
  • 2008 - Forms Order of the Golden Mirror with Nick Stanifer (artist name Jang)
  • 2010 - Records under the band name DAYS with Melissa Netherwood in Sacramento
  • 2012 - Enlisted in the US Army as an infantryman, marries Melissa Netherwood and relocates to Fort Hood, Texas
  • 2013 - Sierra Phillips, his only child is born. Purchases his first home with Melissa Phillips (Netherwood) in Killeen, Texas. Deploys to Afghanistan soon after
  • 2015 - Deploys to South Korea
  • 2016 - Forms the synthesizer-based band, Binary Romance with Melissa Phillips
